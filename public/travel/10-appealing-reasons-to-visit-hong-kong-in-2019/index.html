10 Appealing Reasons To Visit Hong Kong in 2019

Trustle · 2019.5.5

Hong Kong is a great city to visit because it never sleeps, offers great value and a lot of enjoyment. Apart from beautiful skyscrapers and mouth-watering food choices, these are alertnative reasons to visit Hong Kong.

1. Home to a lot of Skyscrapers

We know there are now a lot of skyscrapers built in a lot of other countries but as you might know that Hong Kong itself is a small city but the amount of skyscrapers that can be found is amazing! They are also really beautiful.

2. Loads of Hiking Trails

If you like hiking then visiting Hong Kong is something you should add to your bucked list because they offer a lot of hiking tails and the view offered from them is ridiculously pretty.

3. Great Variety of Food

Hong Kong is a city where not only one type of national live, it’s a united city and it offers a ton of different cuisines such as Chinese, Thai or Indian. It’s ultimately the paradise of food.

4. Convenient Transport

As a citizen living in Hong Kong, the transportation network in Hong Kong is very superior. MTR (Railway System) is connected to every sub city in Hong Kong and the rides are very quick and cheap. Moreover, there are double deckers buses, taxis, Uber rides and great trams.

5. Best Theme Parks

The best part about travelling to Hong Kong is visiting the fun areas that is offered by the country. Therefore, Hong Kong is a great choice because it has Disneyland and Ocean Park. Disneyland is where you can experience Disney characters and at Ocean Park, you learn about aquatic life such as seeing rare pandas.

6. Shopping Paradise

Who doesn’t love shopping? I guess you probably do! That’s great, Hong Kong has a lot of different and famous brands such as Victoria Secret, G200, Gucci, H&M, Zara and a lot more.

Tip for tourist: Try to know a bit of Cantonese, like saying ‘Hi’ since that way the shoppers will think you are a local and may give you discount. (Works best in night markets)

7. Beautiful Temples

Have you ever heard about Big Buddha? If you did then that’s awesome because in Hong Kong, you get to visit a really great place like that. Hong Kong has a lot of temples and they look really charming.

8. Amazing Festivals

There are tons of different festivals celebrated in Hong Kong, even the famous ones like Christmas, Halloween. If you plan your trip during festival season, you may experience dope stuff.

9. Very Interesting History

It’s good to learn something while you are travelling since you can tell your loved ones or friends about it when you go back home. Hong Kong’s history is really fascinating and fun to actually learn more about. I personally enjoyed learning about it not because I’m a citizen in Hong Kong, It’s the fact that I love history.

10. Exciting Clubs to Enjoy

Hong Kong is actually very famous for it’s clubs, especially “Lan Kwai Fong” or LKF in abbreviation. It’s really fun and you get to experience one of the best night life excitement the world.
